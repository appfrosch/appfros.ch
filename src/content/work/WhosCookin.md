## Abstract
In my family of four, me and my wife are in the lucky position that our kids are interested in cooking as much as we are. Also, they have reached an age where they can handle cooking themselves quite well.

At some point my wife started weekly meal planning on a whiteboard. We defined who was cooking what on which day of the coming week.

That was a great start, and while the planning and cooking itself went quite well, the need for a digital solution, where we could share these informations with each other on our individual iPhones became obvious quite quickly.

This is when I started WhosCooking to do exactly that.

## State of the app
**In develpment**

We have been using the app for our family's meal planning now for almost half a year. It's not perfect and still has some bugs, but overall, I am very satisfied with it so far.

The next step will be to fix those bugs and make the app ready for TestFlight and then for the store itself.
