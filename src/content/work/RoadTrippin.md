### About the app

RoadTrippin is a road trip planner for iOS and Android. Add your stops, see them on a map with driving routes and travel times, jot down notes, and keep a packing list — everything synced across your devices.

Trips can be shared with travel companions, who can view or co-edit the itinerary in real time.

### Features

#### 🗺️ Trips & Stations
Create a trip with a title and date range, then add as many stations as you like — campsites, towns, landmarks, petrol stops. Drag to reorder, set per-station date ranges, and give each stop free-text notes.

#### 📍 Maps & Routes
Every station with a location appears on the trip map. RoadTrippin automatically calculates the driving route between consecutive stations and shows the distance and estimated travel time for each leg.

#### ✅ Packing List
Each trip has its own packing list with checkboxes. Checked items collapse into a done section so the remaining items stay in focus.

#### 🤝 Sharing
Invite a travel companion by email. Assign them a role — viewer or editor — and they see the trip in their own app. Admins can change roles or revoke access at any time.

#### 🔄 Offline Support
Trip data is cached locally. If you lose connectivity, your itinerary stays accessible; changes sync automatically when you're back online.

### 🛡️ Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026

#### 📦 Data we store
RoadTrippin stores your email address and the trip data you create (trips, stations, notes, packing lists) on servers provided by [Supabase](https://supabase.com). This data is necessary to sync your trips across devices and to enable sharing with other users.

#### 🔐 Authentication
Sign-up and sign-in are handled by Supabase Auth. Passwords are never stored in plain text. Session tokens are kept in secure local storage on your device.

#### 📵 No tracking
RoadTrippin currently uses no analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party tracking of any kind. We may introduce limited analytics in the future to help improve the app; if we do, we'll update this policy first.

#### 🗑️ Data deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time. For now this is a manual process: email us at splash@appfros.ch and we'll remove your account and everything tied to it. Deletion is permanent. In-app self-service deletion is a planned feature.

#### 🍪 Local storage
A local database on your device caches your trip data for offline use. This data is only accessible by the app and is removed when you delete the app or your account.
