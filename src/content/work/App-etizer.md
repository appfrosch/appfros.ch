## Abstract
Oftentimes I come up with ideas for apps, features or solutions for a code problem that was bugging me for ages at very random times and places. More often than I'd like to admit I asked myself: how should I make a note for myself on this in order to not forget that thought?

Even though there already were (and still are) some apps that could help with that, I thought this could be something that I could look at and create a solution for that fits my needs more closely.

## State of the app
**In develpment**
I am currently implementing the MVP of the app. It is my goal to start testing it with select persons via TestFlight soon. After that, I will look into opening up the TestFlight for more people before finally deploying it to the "real" Apple App Store.

## Feature Set
- **handle project, features, work items and milestones**
- **iCloud synchronization**
- **cross-platform within the Apple ecosystem**
- **add screenshots and audio notes to a feature or work item**
