## Abstract
Classic southern German dice app, where two dice are rolled in turn. Each player must have a higher value than their predecessor–or they loose. 

## State of the app
**In develpment**
I am currently implementing the MVP of the app. It is my goal to start testing it with select persons via TestFlight soon. After that, I will look into opening up the TestFlight for more people before finally deploying it to the "real" Apple App Store.
